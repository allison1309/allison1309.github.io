About Us

Free Snacks is an improv team in Washington, D.C. that was formed over a joint love for Cheez-Its and chocolate covered almonds. We are made up of Adam, Allison, Brendan, Kate, Sean, Spencer, and maybe Ryan!

We endeavor to bring emotional, grounded scenes to the stage, whilst still digging deep into little absurdities. Free Snacks has been playing in D.C. for over a year and is excited to bring you a new, never-before and never-again-seen show!
