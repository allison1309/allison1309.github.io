About Us

Free Snacks is an improv team in Washington, D.C. that was formed over a joint love for Cheez-Its and chocolate covered almonds.
We endeavor to bring emotional, grounded scenes to life, whilst still revelling in life's little absurdities.
Free Snacks has been playing in D.C. for over a year and is excited to bring you a new, never-before and never-again-seen show!
