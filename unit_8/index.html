Free Snacks

About Us

Free Snacks is an improv team in Washington, D.C. that was formed through a mutual love for pizza, bagels, pizza-bagels, Cheez-Its, and chocolate covered almonds.

We slowly build grounded, emotional scenes whilst still digging deep into life's little absurdities. Free Snacks has been playing in D.C. for over a year and is excited to bring you a new, never-before and never-again-seen show!
